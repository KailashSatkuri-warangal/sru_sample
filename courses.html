    • Eligibility Criteria for B.Tech. Admission

  • Candidate should obtain at least 55% marks in 10+2 (Telangana students) and 50% marks (students from other states) in the subjects specified taken together.
  • Students with Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics (PCM) are eligible for all B.Tech. programs.
  • Candidates have to be successful in SRSAT (SR Scholastic Assessment Test)/ JEE-Main/ State Level Engineering Entrance Exams across India including EAPCET(EAMCET)/ Merit in Sports/ Cultural Activities.
