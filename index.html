SRU most commonly refers to SR University, a private university in Telangana, India, known for innovation in tech/engineering, strong industry ties (like with major tech firms for AI/CSE), and an entrepreneurial ecosystem (SRiX) with high placements. It's part of the large Sri Rajeshwara Educational Society conglomerate, focuses on research, global partnerships, and offers programs in CSE, AI, IoT, etc., with strong NIRF rankings.

Innovative Curriculum for the Next Generation Graduates

SR University approaches education with a modern mindset. Along with academic excellence, our curriculum gives students a taste of the real world; with enriching, innovative entrepreneurial learnings. Each course offered here facilitates out-of-the-box thinking and application of theoretical concepts to real world challenges. Inter-Disciplinary learning ensures that our students gain knowledge and perspective over multiple subjects. SR University innovative curriculum is designed based on four core values.
